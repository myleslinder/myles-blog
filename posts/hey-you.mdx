# The Great Reset

What if a genie appeared at your door and offered you a free year to spend as you wish? Well, there's some fine print about what you _can't_ use it for, covid causes this so it means you cannot spend the year travelling, or attending conferences, and within whatever you choose some limits apply as well. Would it even come to mind for you to ask "what's the return policy?", would you be overwhelmed with indecision?
